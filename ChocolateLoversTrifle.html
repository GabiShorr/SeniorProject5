My Famous Chocolate Lovers Trifle

Betty Crocker Chocolate Cake Mix

Ingredients

Betty Crocker Cake Mix
Chocolate Pudding (follow the recipe on thePudding page)
Cool Whip
M&M's
Heath Bar Crunch

Steps:

STEP 1: Follow the recipe on the Betty Crocker Cake Mix, bake cakes and let cool completely
STEP 2: Follow the recipe on the pudding box
STEP 3: Start layering the ingredients in a trifle dish but if you don't have that then a bowl works perfectly, Cake then pudding then Cool Whip then the Heath Bar and M&M's
STEP 4: Repeat layering twice, make sure M&M's and Heath Bar are on top!
STEP 5: Enjoy!

*PRO TIP - Add as much or as little of these toppings as you want! This recipe is so easy and delicious for a large group!
