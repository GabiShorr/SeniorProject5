Crepes

For this one, grab a box pancake mix (saves some time!), add extra water to make the batter thinner!

Bisquick Pancake Mix

Ingredients

Bisqick Pancake Mix
1/4 extra water
OPTIONAL - any filling of your choice

Steps:

STEP 1: Follow the pancake mix instructions
STEP 2: Heat pan on a medium heat setting
STEP 3: Cook until slightly brown and flip crepes
STEP 4: Add filling of your choice

*PRO TIP - Sometimes all you need is a pre-made mix, I always encourage making your own however for this the mix is perfect!
