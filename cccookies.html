Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

1 cup butter
2 eggs
3/4 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 heaping tsp vanilla extract
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 bag of chcocolate chips

Steps:

STEP 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees
STEP 2: Mix all wet ingredients in big bowl first (butter, eggs, sugar, vanilla extract)
STEP 3: Add in dry ingredients (flour, baking soda, salt)
STEP 4: Mash together in separate bowl (bananas, sour cream, cinnamon, (nutmeg))
STEP 5: Combine everything together
STEP 6: Put in bread pan, bake for 60 mins or until center is cooked

*PRO TIP - If you are limited on space and supplies or are feeling lazy (like me) you can combine everything in 1 bowl!
