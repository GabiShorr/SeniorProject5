Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

1 cup butter
2 eggs
3/4 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 heaping tsp vanilla extract
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 bag of chocolate chips

Steps:

STEP 1: Preheat oven to 375 degrees
STEP 2: Brown the butter in a sauce pan until slightly golden in color
STEP 3: Combine wet ingredients in large bowl (butter,eggs,sugar,vanilla)
STEP 4: Slowly incorperate dry ingredients into large bowl with wet ingredients
STEP 5: Beat everything until fully combined
STEP 6: Stir in chocolate chips
STEP 6: Scoop balls of dough on baking sheet that is greased or has parchment paper
STEP 6: Bake for 11-15 minutes depending on size of cookie
STEP 6: Let cookies cook on a cooling rack for a few minutes to set then enjoy!!

*PRO TIP - Look for the edges to be golden before taking the cookies out of the oven!
