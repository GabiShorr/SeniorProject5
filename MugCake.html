Microwave Mug Cake

By: AllRecipes.com

Ingredients

¼ cup all-purpose flour
¼ cup white sugar
2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
⅛ teaspoon baking soda
⅛ teaspoon salt
3 tablespoons milk
2 tablespoons canola oil
¼ teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps:

STEP 1: Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large microwave-safe mug; stir in milk, canola oil, water, and vanilla extract.
STEP 2: Cook in the microwave until cake is done in the middle, about 1 minute 45 seconds.

*PRO TIP - Use something that can go into the microwave; like a mug! Also canola oil can be substituted for vegetable oil.
