Microwave Mug Cookie

By: Tasty.co

Ingredients - 1 serving

2 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 tablespoon sugar
¼ teaspoon baking powder
⅛ teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
3 tablespoons flour
2 tablespoons chocolate chips
OPTIONAL - ice cream!

Steps:

STEP 1: Add 2 tbsp butter, 1 tbsp brown sugar, 1 tbsp sugar, ¼ tsp baking powder, ⅛ tsp salt and ¼ tsp vanilla in a bowl and mix.
STEP 2: Next, add 3 tbsp flour and 2 tbsp chocolate chips and mix thoroughly. Transfer the mix to a microwave-friendly bowl and heat for 60 seconds. Top with ice cream.
STEP 3: Enjoy!

*PRO TIP - Double ingredients for more cookie!
