Brownies

By: AllRecipes.com

Ingredients

2 cups white sugar
1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
1 cup butter, melted
4 eggs
½ cup cocoa powder
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
½ teaspoon baking powder
½ teaspoon salt
½ cup walnut halves

Steps:

STEP 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
STEP 2: Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined. Spread the batter into the prepared pan. Decorate with walnut halves.
STEP 3: Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 20 to 30 minutes; cool before slicing into squares.

*PRO TIP - Walnuts are optional, I usually replace them with chocolate chips!
